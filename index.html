M U F F L E R

Double cloth or double weave is a kind of woven textile in which two or more sets of warps or one or more sets of weft or filling yarns are interconnected to form a two layered cloth .The movement of threads between the layers allow complex patterns and surface textures to be created.
Double weaving is an ancient technique. Surviving examples from the Parcas culture of Peru have been dated to before AD 700. Double face fabrics are a form of double cloth made of onw warp and two sets of wefts, or (less often) two warps of one weft.These fabrics have two right sides and have no wrong sides ,include most blankets, satin ribbons, and interlinings
Modern applications of double cloth include haute couture coats, blankets, furnishing fabrics, and some brocades.
